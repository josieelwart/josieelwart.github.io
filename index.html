About


At Levata, we believe everyone deserves a home that inspires, connects, & brings rest through thoughtful design.

  • What We Offer


    Levata is a residential interior design firm in the heart of Nashville, TN that specializes in Scandinavian design with a cozy modern approach to every space.

  • Our Goals


    We take our time to know our clientsWe treat everyone like familyWe listen to our clients wants & needsWe design for everyone’s personality for their own unique design

  • Our Mission

    Our mission is to create a respite for any home in a modern and comfortable way.
